Concept Introduction

In smart homes, a command is a single task that a smart device will execute. For example, "Turn on the light" is a command for a smart light.

In smart homes, a routine is defined as a set/sequence of commands. As stated in the previous section, a command is a single task that a smart device will execute.


Purpose of The Survey

In our tagging system, you will have the opportunity to "tag" routines and/or commands, meaning you can assign a word or phrase to them to convey what the purpose of the routine is, what is important about the routine to you, or how you plan on differentiating other routines from it. For example, you can mark a routine as "energy saving" if it turns off the air conditioner in your house, or as "safety" if it locks your door.
